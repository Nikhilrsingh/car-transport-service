Important Tips

Remove Personal Items:

Personal belongings are not covered by transport insurance. Remove all items from your vehicle, including toll tags and garage door openers.

Document Condition:

Take detailed photos of your vehicle from all angles before transport. This helps with any insurance claims if needed.

Spare Keys:

Provide a spare key to the driver. Make sure all keys and remotes are clearly labeled and working.

Stay Reachable:

Keep your phone accessible. We'll contact you for pickup and delivery coordination.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our process

How much does car transport cost?

Costs vary based on distance, vehicle type, and transport method. Use our instant quote calculator for accurate pricing. Typical ranges are $500-$1500 for most routes.

Is my vehicle insured during transport?

Yes! All vehicles are covered by our comprehensive carrier insurance. Additional coverage options are available for high-value vehicles.

Can I put personal items in my car?

We recommend removing all personal items as they are not covered by insurance. Some carriers may allow up to 100 lbs in the trunk, but this varies.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and bank transfers. A deposit is required at booking, with the balance due at delivery.

Do you transport non-running vehicles?

Yes, we can transport non-running vehicles using specialized equipment. Additional fees may apply. Please mention this when getting your quote.

Can I track my vehicle during transport?

Absolutely! We provide real-time GPS tracking and regular updates via SMS and email throughout the transport journey.
