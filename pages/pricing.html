Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the Basic plan?

The Basic plan includes:

  • Door-to-door pickup and delivery service
  • Insurance coverage up to ₹50,000
  • Standard delivery timeline (5-7 days)
  • Email and phone support during business hours
  • Basic vehicle inspection before and after transport

Perfect for hatchbacks and small cars on a budget.

How does GPS tracking work in the Standard plan?

With our Standard and Premium plans, you get real-time GPS tracking that allows you to:

  • Monitor your vehicle's location 24/7 through our mobile app or website
  • Receive automatic updates on key milestones
  • View estimated arrival times based on current location
  • Get instant notifications for any route changes

You'll receive a unique tracking ID once your vehicle is loaded.

What makes the Premium plan worth it?

The Premium plan is designed for luxury and high-value vehicles, offering:

  • Enhanced insurance coverage up to ₹10,00,000
  • Enclosed carrier transport only for maximum protection
  • Priority loading and express delivery (2-3 days)
  • Dedicated support manager assigned to your shipment
  • VIP 24/7 customer support
  • Climate-controlled transport options
  • Professional detailing before delivery
Can I upgrade my plan after booking?

Yes! You can upgrade your plan at any time before the vehicle is picked up. Simply contact our customer support team, and they'll help you upgrade to a higher plan. The price difference will be calculated, and you'll receive an updated invoice.

Note: Downgrades are only available within 24 hours of booking and may be subject to a small processing fee.

How is the insurance coverage calculated?

Our insurance coverage protects your vehicle during transport:

  • Basic Plan: Coverage up to ₹50,000 for damage or loss
  • Standard Plan: Coverage up to ₹2,00,000 with comprehensive protection
  • Premium Plan: Coverage up to ₹10,00,000 with zero-deductible policy

All plans include third-party liability coverage. You can also purchase additional insurance if your vehicle's value exceeds the plan limits.

What's the difference between Local and Interstate pricing?

We offer two pricing tiers based on distance:

  • Local (Under 500km): Lower base rates for shorter distances within the same region
  • Interstate (500km+): Optimized rates for long-distance transport with volume discounts. Save up to 15% compared to local per-km rates!
