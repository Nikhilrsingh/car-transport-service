What to Do in Case of Emergency

Step-by-step guides for common emergency situations

1

In Case of Accident

  • Ensure everyone's safety first
  • Call emergency services if needed (911)
  • Contact our emergency hotline immediately
  • Take photos of the scene and damage
  • Do not move vehicles unless necessary
  • Exchange information with other parties
  • File a police report
2

Delivery Delays

  • Check real-time tracking for updates
  • Contact customer support for status
  • Review your delivery timeline
  • Request revised ETA from our team
  • Document delay for insurance purposes
  • Discuss compensation if applicable
3

Vehicle Damage

  • Inspect vehicle upon delivery
  • Document all damage with photos
  • Note damage on delivery receipt
  • Contact us within 24 hours
  • File insurance claim immediately
  • Keep all documentation safe
  • Get repair estimates from certified shops
4

Vehicle Breakdown

  • Move to safe location if possible
  • Call roadside assistance immediately
  • Provide exact location details
  • Stay with vehicle until help arrives
  • Follow driver's safety instructions
  • Get estimated repair time

Emergency Support FAQs

What qualifies as an emergency?

Emergencies include accidents, vehicle breakdowns, significant delays, vehicle damage, safety concerns, or any situation requiring immediate assistance.

How quickly will someone respond?

Emergency calls are answered immediately 24/7. Critical situations receive response within 15-30 minutes. Non-critical emergencies within 1-2 hours.

Is emergency support free?

Emergency support is included in all transport services. Roadside assistance may have additional costs depending on the service required.

What if I can't reach anyone?

If you cannot reach us (unlikely), call 911 for immediate safety concerns, then try our backup emergency number or submit an emergency form online.

Can I track my emergency request?

Yes, all emergency requests receive a tracking number. You'll get SMS and email updates throughout the resolution process.

What information should I have ready?

Have your booking ID, current location, contact information, and a description of the emergency ready when you call or chat.
