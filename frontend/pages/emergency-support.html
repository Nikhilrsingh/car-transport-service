In Case of Accident

First Action

Ensure everyone's safety first

Vehicle Damage

First Action

Inspect vehicle thoroughly upon delivery

  1. Take photos of all damage immediately
  2. Note damage on delivery receipt
  3. Call us within 24 hours at +919372693389
  4. File insurance claim right away

First Action

Move to safe location if possible

  2. Provide exact location details
